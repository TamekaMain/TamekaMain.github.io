Previous Projects

Displayed below are some projects I have been able to ideate and create throughout my degree, feel free to take some inspiration from these projects for your own classroom.

Past Projects

Timber Footstool design project -3 legged stool that has been designed for Taronga zoo with an aesthetic upholstery design.

Textile Banner project - inspired by monets water lilies art work, the flowers centre has lights that are programmed to flash pink

Honey Comb pajama pattern / project - Learning to use a sewing machine, look at patters and make pajama pants with a screenprinted design for the t-shirt.

Timber Lamp project - Collapsable lamp design with laser cut pannels and a fitted top, the pannels are magnetised to the base of the lamp.

Food Photography - Aesthetically placed garnishes of dips and crackers using back splash boards to create an appealing image, learning about the important aspects of food photography.

Engineering project - Laser cut rubberband powered racecar aesthetically designed like a shoe with a timber robot man that sits in the racecar. Purpose of learning about energy and force from a rubberband.
