Projects completed during my course

These are some work samples and projects I have completed during my degree:

Industrial timber Footstool project

Timber footstool design project - 3 legged stool that has been designed for Taronga zoo with an aesthetic upholstery design

Textile banner with lights project

Textile banner project inspired by monets water lilies art work, the flowers centre has lights that are programmed to flash pink

Food technology photography project

Aesthetically placed garnishes of dips and crackers using back splash boards to create an appealing image, learning about the important aspects of food photography.

Design in timber lamp project

Collapsable lamp design with laser cut pannels and a fitted top, the pannels are magnetised to the base of the lamp.

Electromechanical rubberband racecar project

Laser cut rubberband powered racecar aesthetically designed like a shoe with a timber robot man that sits in the racecar.

Textiles honeycomb pajamas project

Learning to use a sewing machine, look at patters and make pajama pants with a screenprinted design for the t-shirt.
