Resources

This collection of resources provides a comprehensive overview of teaching materials across Stage 5 and Stage 6 in Design and Technologies and Food Technology. It includes a Stage 6 Food Product Development + Design and Technologies Sample Unit of Work, offering structured guidance for senior students. The Stage 5 Year 10 resource, Reasons for Cooking Food, supports foundational understanding of food preparation principles. For practical design skills, the Stage 5 Timber + Design and Technologies Stool Project Drawing aligns with AS1100 drawing standards, reinforcing technical drawing competencies. Additionally, the Stage 6 Design and Technologies - Conversions and Seasoning of Timber Questions resource promotes deeper theoretical understanding of material properties, essential for advanced study and project development. Together, these resources support both theoretical knowledge and practical skill-building across key learning stages.
